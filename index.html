About Us

Olive Valley Trading (Pty) Ltd is a Mahikeng-based company offering sustainability consulting, climate resilience advisory, research services, and technical innovation support. Founded by Dr. Kealeboga Mmolutsi, the company is committed to equity-driven and evidence-based solutions for vulnerable communities and public infrastructure.

Our Services

Sustainability & Climate Resilience Advisory

We provide integrated green infrastructure, circular retrofitting, environmental monitoring, and policy alignment solutions.

Incidents Evaluation & Human Factor Analysis

Investigating environmental and operational risk interactions in settlements adjacent to sensitive infrastructure zones.

Research & Advisory Services

Socioeconomic impact studies, housing needs assessments, and strategy development for inclusive urban resilience.

Grant Writing & Strategic Fundraising

We support innovators and organizations in developing compelling proposals for local and international funding opportunities.

Flagship Initiative

Green Resilience Initiatives for Airport-Adjacent Communities
